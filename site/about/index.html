About AI Market Pulse

AI jobs, market intelligence, and tools for AI professionals.

Our Mission

AI Market Pulse helps AI professionals navigate their careers with real data — not recruiter spin, not hype, not LinkedIn fluff.

We built this because we needed it ourselves. Finding accurate salary data, understanding which AI tools actually work, and tracking the rapidly evolving job market was harder than it should be. So we created a resource where AI professionals can find jobs, compare tools, and stay informed on market trends.

What We Offer

  • Job Board: Curated AI roles updated weekly. Prompt Engineer, AI Engineer, ML Engineer, MLOps, and more. We show real salary ranges when available.
  • Salary Data: Transparent compensation data for AI roles across companies, locations, and experience levels.
  • Tool Reviews: Honest assessments of AI development tools. No sponsored content — we recommend what we actually use.
  • Market Intelligence: Weekly analysis of the AI job market through our AI News Digest newsletter.
  • AI Consulting: Strategy and implementation services for startups and tech companies looking to integrate AI into their GTM operations.
